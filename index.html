TeamTreehouse

Last, but definitely not least (it is actually no.1 for me) Treehouse. This site is truly something else. So much to learn, so much to see/try than I often found myself jumping tracks just to see quicker what I'll learn down the road. The entire website as well as the teachers just inspire confidence. It's got it's own code editor ("Workspaces") which can be used any time. Their social-media presence is also great, and as such they are able to post motivational quotes/pictures/stories weekly, which are much needed for a "toddler" aspiring developer.
